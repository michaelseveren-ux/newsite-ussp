## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Homepage section order
The homepage SHALL display sections in the following order from top to bottom:
1. Hero
2. Services ("Our Services")
3. Featured Aircraft
4. Why Choose Us
5. Get in Touch (Contact)

#### Scenario: Visitor views homepage sections in order
- **WHEN** a visitor loads the homepage and scrolls down
- **THEN** they see Hero first, followed by Services, then Featured Aircraft, then Why Choose Us, then Get in Touch
